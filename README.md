# credit-card-analytics-dashboard
Power BI dashboard analyzing credit card transactions, customer behavior, and risk (delinquency).

## 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze credit card customer data to understand spending behavior, identify high-value customer segments, and assess credit risk using delinquency patterns.

The dashboard helps businesses make data-driven decisions by providing insights into revenue trends, customer demographics, and transaction behavior.

## 💡 Key Insights

* High-income customers contribute nearly **49% of total revenue**, making them the most valuable segment.
* The **Blue card category** generates the highest revenue among all card types.
* Customers aged **40–60 years** are the highest contributors to revenue.
* The delinquency rate is approximately **6%**, indicating moderate credit risk.
* Most transactions are done via **Swipe**, showing dominant transaction behavior.
* Bills and entertainment are the top spending categories.
